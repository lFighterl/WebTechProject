Marvel’s Agents Of S.H.I.E.L.D

Marvel's Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., or simply Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., is an American television series created for ABC based on the Marvel Comics organization S.H.I.E.L.D.

Phil coulsoon

Phil coulson played by clark gregg. A S.H.I.E.L.D. agent who oversees many of the division's field operations.

He served as Director Fury's second-in-command for some of the Division's most important missions. One of S.H.I.E.L.D.'s most decorated operatives, Coulson was put in charge of surveillance and served as the primary operative in the Avengers Initiative.
